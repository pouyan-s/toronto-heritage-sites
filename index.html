Heritage Sites in Toronto’s Neighborhoods

Curated by Pouyan Shahidi
This map displays the locations of heritage sites across Toronto, overlaid on the city’s official neighborhood boundaries. The number of heritage sites within each neighborhood is represented using graduated shades of blue. Click on any neighborhood or heritage site to view detailed information.

Data Sources
1. City of Toronto. Heritage Register. Open Data Portal, 1998–2024, open.toronto.ca/dataset/heritage-register/. Accessed 15 Feb. 2025.
2. City of Toronto. Neighbourhoods. Open Data Portal, 1998–2025, open.toronto.ca/dataset/neighbourhoods/. Accessed 15 Feb. 2025.
